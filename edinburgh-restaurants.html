Are you hungry?

Culinary Delights at One Square

One Square is the hotel’s signature dining venue, offering a modern take on Scottish cuisine. The restaurant prides itself on using the freshest local ingredients, ensuring a menu that reflects the best of Scotland’s natural larder.

One Square Brasserie

Open from morning to night, the Brasserie offers a relaxed atmosphere to enjoy seasonal dishes crafted with locally sourced produce.

One Square Bar

Known for its extensive selection of over 100 gins, including the house-crafted One Square Gin, the bar provides a sophisticated setting for drinks and light bites.

Weekend Champagne Brunch

Indulge in a two-course brunch accompanied by La Garde Écossaise Brut Champagne, featuring a sharing platter and a choice of delightful brunch dishes.

Afternoon Tea

Celebrate the hotel’s 40th anniversary with a themed afternoon tea, offering a selection of teas served in unique ‘Tea-iere’ infusers and an array of sweet and savory treats.

Endless Sunday Lunch

Enjoy a carvery-style feast with live jazz, including starters, sides, and an extensive dessert selection, available on the first Sunday of each month.
