Sheraton Grand Hotel & Spa, Edinburgh

Hotel Details

Experience Modern Scottish Luxury

Located in the heart of Edinburgh, the Sheraton Grand Hotel & Spa offers a blend of contemporary comfort and traditional Scottish hospitality. Situated near iconic landmarks such as Edinburgh Castle and the Royal Mile, the hotel provides an ideal base for exploring the city’s rich heritage.

Amenities and Services

  • One Spa: An award-winning spa featuring a rooftop Hydropool, Thermal Suite, and a variety of treatments designed to rejuvenate the body and mind.
  • Fitness Centre: State-of-the-art gym facilities equipped with the latest exercise equipment.
  • Indoor Pool: A 19-meter infinity-edge pool, perfect for both relaxation and exercise.
