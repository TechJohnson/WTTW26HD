InterContinental Tahiti Resort & Spa

Hotel Details

Experience Polynesian Luxury

Nestled on the western coast of Tahiti, the InterContinental Tahiti Resort & Spa offers an idyllic retreat with stunning views of Moorea across the lagoon. The resort seamlessly blends contemporary comfort with traditional Polynesian design, providing guests with an authentic island experience.

Amenities and Activities

  • Lagoonarium: Discover a protected marine habitat teeming with colorful fish, perfect for snorkeling enthusiasts.
  • Infinity Pools: Relax in two freshwater infinity pools overlooking the lagoon, providing a serene setting for unwinding.
  • Deep Nature Spa: Indulge in rejuvenating treatments inspired by Polynesian traditions, utilizing natural ingredients to revitalize the body and mind.
  • Water Sports: Engage in various activities, including kayaking, paddleboarding, and jet skiing, all available directly from the resort’s beachfront.
  • Cultural Demonstrations: Participate in traditional Polynesian crafts, dance performances, and cooking classes to deepen your understanding of local culture.
