Culinary Delights for Every Palate

Planet Hollywood Cancun offers a gastronomic journey through its diverse dining venues, each providing a unique ambiance and menu:

So Cal Gourmet Emporium

A buffet-style restaurant featuring a fusion of Southern California flavors, including wood-fired flatbreads, sushi, and Mexican street food.

Guy’s! Burger Joint

Celebrity chef Guy Fieri brings his signature burgers and seasoned fries to this casual, open-air eatery.

East Sushi & Teppanyaki Bar

Experience interactive dining with skilled chefs preparing sushi and teppanyaki dishes right before your eyes.

Sunset Strip Steakhouse & Grill

An intimate steakhouse offering premium cuts and an extensive wine list in a Hollywood-inspired setting.

Gusto Italian Trattoria

Savor classic Italian dishes like pasta, risotto, and pizza in a romantic, candlelit atmosphere.

Sutra Indian Restaurant & Hookah Lounge

Delight in authentic Indian cuisine and a variety of hookah flavors in a vibrant, Bollywood-themed environment.

La Cocina Mexican Cuisine

Enjoy traditional Mexican dishes crafted with fresh, local ingredients, complemented by ocean views.

The Shores Beach Club & Restaurant

A beachfront venue serving grilled specialties and refreshing beverages, perfect for a relaxed meal by the sea.

Overtime Sports Bar

Catch live sports events on big screens while enjoying classic pub fare and a wide selection of drinks.
