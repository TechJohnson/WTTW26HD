Wyndham Grand Barbados Sam Lord’s Castle

Hotel Details

Experience Luxury Amidst Historic Charm

Perched on the southeastern coast of Barbados, the Wyndham Grand Barbados Sam Lord’s Castle All-Inclusive Resort offers a harmonious blend of modern luxury and historical intrigue. Adjacent to the storied ruins of Sam Lord’s Castle, the resort provides guests with a unique opportunity to immerse themselves in the island’s rich past while enjoying contemporary comforts.

Amenities and Services

  • Dining: A variety of on-site restaurants and bars offer diverse culinary experiences, from gourmet seafood to traditional Caribbean fare.
  • Pools: Multiple outdoor pools, including an infinity pool, provide refreshing retreats with stunning ocean vistas.
  • Spa: The resort’s spa offers a range of treatments designed to rejuvenate the body and mind.
  • Fitness Center: Stay active with state-of-the-art equipment and wellness classes.
  • Activities: Engage in tennis, pickleball, and a variety of organized events and entertainment options.
