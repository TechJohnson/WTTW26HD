Hotel Dubrovnik Palace

Hotel Details

Experience Luxury by the Adriatic

Perched on the scenic Lapad Peninsula, Hotel Dubrovnik Palace offers a serene retreat with panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea and the Elaphiti Islands. This five-star resort seamlessly blends modern design with natural beauty, providing guests with an unforgettable stay just minutes from Dubrovnik’s historic center.

Amenities and Services

  • Wellness & Spa: Indulge in rejuvenating treatments at the award-winning spa, featuring an indoor pool, sauna, steam room, and a range of therapies.
  • Outdoor Pools: Relax by the infinity pool overlooking the sea or enjoy the dedicated children’s pool area.
  • Private Beach: Access a secluded beach area with sun loungers and umbrellas, perfect for soaking up the sun.
  • Fitness Center: Stay active with state-of-the-art gym facilities and outdoor jogging trails.
  • Tennis Courts: Challenge yourself on the hotel’s tennis courts, set amidst lush greenery.
  • Diving Center: Explore the underwater wonders of the Adriatic with diving excursions organized by the on-site PADI-certified center.
