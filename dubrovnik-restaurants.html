Culinary Delights with a View

Hotel Dubrovnik Palace boasts a diverse array of dining venues, each offering delectable cuisine paired with breathtaking sea views. From traditional Dalmatian dishes to health-conscious options, guests can indulge in a variety of culinary experiences without leaving the resort.

Elafiti Restaurant

The hotel’s main restaurant serves a sumptuous buffet breakfast and dinner, featuring Mediterranean and international dishes. Guests can dine indoors or on the terrace, enjoying panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea.

Maslina Tavern

Experience authentic Dubrovnik-style dining at this traditional tavern, offering an à la carte menu of local specialties, including the renowned Fisherman’s Pot. Live music and a romantic ambiance enhance the dining experience.

Lean & Light Restaurant

Located on the 10th floor, this health-focused restaurant offers light meals, fresh salads, and smoothies, catering to guests seeking nutritious options. The venue also provides stunning views of the surrounding seascape.

Beach Restaurant Ponta

Situated by the beach, Ponta offers a relaxed setting for enjoying summer snacks, salads, and grilled dishes, perfect for a casual meal by the sea.

Sunset Lounge

Unwind with a cocktail or a glass of fine wine at the Sunset Lounge, featuring floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the mesmerizing sunset over the Adriatic.

Lanterna Glorijet Bar

This poolside bar serves a selection of refreshing beverages and light bites, ideal for enjoying a leisurely afternoon by the water.
