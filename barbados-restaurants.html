Culinary Delights to Savor

The resort’s diverse dining options cater to a range of palates, offering both local flavors and international cuisines in settings that range from casual to elegant.

Castle View Restaurant

An adults-only fine dining venue overlooking the historic castle ruins, serving sophisticated dishes in an intimate atmosphere.

Mediterranean Market

A buffet-style restaurant offering a variety of Mediterranean and international dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Sam Lord’s Grill

A casual eatery providing a selection of grilled favorites and comfort foods, perfect for a relaxed meal.

Lucy’s Lounge

A vibrant spot to enjoy cocktails, light bites, and live entertainment in a lively setting.

Lantern Bar

A poolside bar offering refreshing beverages and snacks, ideal for a midday break or sunset drink.

Burnt Cask Rum Bar

Specializing in premium Caribbean rums, this bar provides a cozy ambiance for evening relaxation and tastings.
