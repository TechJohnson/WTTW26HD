Planet Hollywood Cancun, An Autograph Collection All-Inclusive Resort

Hotel Details

Experience the Glamour of Hollywood on the Shores of the Caribbean

Step into the spotlight at Planet Hollywood Cancun, where you don’t just stay — you star. Nestled along the pristine beaches of Costa Mujeres, this all-inclusive resort invites you to Vacation Like a Star™ with luxurious accommodations, world-class amenities, and curated experiences that elevate your escape into something truly cinematic.

From the moment you arrive, you’re immersed in the world of fame and film. Authentic Hollywood memorabilia lines the halls, red carpet moments await, and the energy of stardom pulses throughout the resort. Whether you’re unwinding in your stylish room, sipping cocktails by a glittering pool, or indulging in global cuisine, every moment is designed to make you feel like an A-lister.

Amenities and Services

  • Dining: Eleven diverse restaurants and eleven bars, offering global cuisines and signature cocktails.
  • Pools and Water Features: Multiple pools, a lazy river, and a FlowRider® surf simulator provide endless aquatic fun.
  • Entertainment: Daily and nightly shows, themed events, and a state-of-the-art cinema keep guests entertained.
  • Wellness: The PH Spa & Beauty Bar offers rejuvenating treatments, while the PUMPED Fitness Center caters to fitness enthusiasts.
  • Kids and Teens: The Stars Kids Club and Planet Play Adventure Park ensure younger guests have their own adventures.
  • Shopping: On-site boutiques and souvenir shops provide retail therapy without leaving the resort.
