Are you hungry?

The resort boasts a selection of dining venues that cater to diverse palates, offering both international cuisine and local specialties in picturesque settings.

Le Lotus Restaurant

Perched over the lagoon, Le Lotus offers an intimate dining experience with panoramic views of Moorea. The menu features gourmet French cuisine crafted with local ingredients, complemented by an extensive wine list.

Te Tiare Restaurant

As the resort’s main dining venue, Te Tiare serves a variety of dishes throughout the day. Enjoy breakfast buffets, à la carte lunches, and themed dinners showcasing Polynesian flavors. The restaurant also hosts cultural shows on select evenings, enhancing the dining experience with traditional music and dance.

Tiki Bar

Located near the main pool, the Tiki Bar offers a relaxed atmosphere for enjoying tropical cocktails and light snacks. Live music performances create a lively ambiance, making it a perfect spot for evening gatherings.

Le Lotus Swim-Up Bar

For a unique experience, swim up to this bar situated within the sand-bottomed pool. Sip on refreshing beverages without leaving the water, all while soaking in the stunning surroundings.

Lobby Bar Terrace

Open 24 hours, the Lobby Bar Terrace provides a comfortable setting for casual drinks and light meals. Its location offers beautiful views of the lagoon, making it an ideal spot for both early morning coffee and late-night cocktails.
