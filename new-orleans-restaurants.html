Savor the Flavors of New Orleans

Hotel Monteleone presents exceptional dining experiences that celebrate the culinary richness of the region.

Criollo Restaurant

Embodying the fusion of European and Caribbean influences, Criollo offers a menu that highlights the freshest local ingredients. Guests can enjoy breakfast, lunch, and dinner in an elegant setting, with specialties that pay homage to New Orleans’ diverse culinary heritage.

Le Petit Chef

An immersive dining experience, Le Petit Chef combines 3D visual storytelling with a five-course meal, taking guests on a gastronomic journey that’s both entertaining and delicious.

Carousel Bar & Lounge

As New Orleans’ only revolving bar, the Carousel Bar offers a whimsical atmosphere where guests can enjoy classic cocktails like the Vieux Carré, created right at this very bar. Live music and a curated bar bites menu enhance the vibrant experience.
